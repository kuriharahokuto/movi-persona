# Branding Guide

Version: 1.0

## Product Name

MOVI Persona

Your Life, Your Movie

---

# Brand Mission

Help users discover:

"What kind of movie protagonist am I?"

through cinematic decision-making experiences.

The service should feel like entering a movie and discovering your role within the story.

---

# Core Concept

MOVI Persona / Your Life, Your Movie

The product is not:

- a movie database
    
- a movie review site
    
- a movie community
    

The primary experience is:

Movie Archetype Discovery

Users should feel they are making choices inside a movie.

---

# Brand Promise

Every user leaves with:

- a movie archetype
    
- a shareable identity
    
- a deeper understanding of themselves
    

The result should feel meaningful and memorable.

---

# Design Philosophy

The experience should feel like:

A movie poster about yourself.

Not:

- a corporate dashboard
    
- a psychological report
    
- a streaming service

# Question Experience

Every question should feel like a movie scene.

Avoid:

- generic personality questions
    
- survey style questions
    

Use:

- narrative scenarios
    
- emotional decisions
    
- cinematic tension
    

---

# Visual Question Design

Display:

- scene title
    
- short situation
    
- immersive artwork
    

Recommended Future Feature

AI-generated scene images

Each question should feel like a frame from a movie.
